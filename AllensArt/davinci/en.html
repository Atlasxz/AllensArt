LEONARDO DI SER PIERO

DA VINCI
Leonardo di Ser Piero da Vinci, or simply Leonardo da Vinci, was a polymath born in present-day Italy, one of the most important figures of the High Renaissance, who stood out as a scientist, mathematician, engineer, inventor, anatomist, painter, sculptor, architect, botanist, poet and musician.
