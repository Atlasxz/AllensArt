Michelangelo

Michelangelo di Ludovico Buonarroti Simoni was born on March 6, 1475, in the Italian city of Caprese, but while still a newborn, he was taken by his parents, Francesca and Ludovico di Leonardo Buonarroti Simoni, to Florence. In this Tuscan city the arts flourished thanks to the efforts of the Medici family. A few meters from Michelangelo's home, Sandro Boticelli (1445-1510) was creating The Spring, Leonardo da Vinci (1452-1519) was painting The Adoration of the Magi, and in nearby Urbino, Raphael Sanzio (1483-1520) was born.
