RENOIR
Renoir was a very important French artist of the late 19th century. He had great prominence in painting and, along with other artists, he continued a new way of brushing, which became known as impressionism. He appreciated the beauty of life and brought optimism, harmony and tranquility to his canvases by painting many outdoor scenes. He once said:

"For me, a painting should be something lovely, joyful and beautiful, yes, beautiful.
There are already many unpleasant things in life. Why do we invent more?"
