VAN GOGH
Van Gogh was a Dutch painter born on March 30, 1853, considered one of the most recent artists, his recognition has been given only after his death. The artist was characterized by some as a misunderstood, tormented, untimely and behaviorally disturbed man. Therefore, not only his works but also his life produce a lot of curiosity these days. Van Gogh has been the subject of several books, films and documentaries.
