OSCAR-CLAUDE

MONET
Oscar-Claude Monet was a French painter and the most famous among Impressionist painters. The term Impressionism arose from a criticism of one of Monet's early paintings, "Impression, sunrise", made to the work by the painter and writer Louis Leroy: "Impression, sunrise” – I knew it! The most striking feature of this artistic school are the paintings in the open air. Alongside Monet, they deserve highlight the impressionist painters: Camille Pisarro, Édouard Manet, Edgar Degas, Alfred Sisley and Pierre-Auguste Renoir.
